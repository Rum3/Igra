# Noir Detective: The Spider's Mesh - Master Plan

## General Description
A text-based detective noir thriller focused on investigation, deduction, and decision-making. The game consists of one main investigation (20 chapters) and 5 side cases (1 chapter each). Players never know if they are on the right track until the very end.

## Main Story: The Spider's Mesh
**Genre:** Noir, Psychological Thriller, Economic & Political Detective.
**Premise:** Investigative journalist Thomas Vane is found dead in his locked office. Ruled a suicide by police, but his widow hires you to find the truth. Vane was uncovering a massive conspiracy involving the underworld, a financial mega-corporation, and high-ranking officials.

---

## Chapter Structure (20 Chapters)

### Part I: Into the Rabbit Hole (Chapters 1–5)
- **Chapter 1: The Locked Room.** Police reports, suicide note, and initial personal effects. (Status: COMPLETED)
- **Chapter 2: Inconsistencies.** Autopsy analysis and initial interrogations (Widow & Assistant). Action Points mechanic introduced. (Status: COMPLETED)
- **Chapter 3: The Last Report.** Recovering deleted files from Vane's laptop. Fragments of chats with source "Spider". (Status: IN PROGRESS)
- **Chapter 4: The First Lead.** Choice between two locations: "The Blackwood" (luxury) or an industrial warehouse.
- **Chapter 5: Alternate Reality.** Encountering a false lead (drugs). Risk of collecting fake evidence.

### Part II: The Corporate Labyrinth (Chapters 6–10)
- **Chapter 6: Atlas Foundation.** Investigation into the powerful investment group Vane was researching.
- **Chapter 7: CEO Interrogation.** Confronting the CEO. Tone selection (Aggressive vs. Stealthy).
- **Chapter 8: The Shadow in the Force.** Anonymous warning from inside the police. Evidence of corruption.
- **Chapter 9: The Red Herring.** A low-level criminal appears as a perfect scapegoat to lure the player into closing the case early.
- **Chapter 10: Blood Money.** Discovering Vane's offshore accounts. Extortion or blackmail?

### Part III: Breaking Point (Chapters 11–15)
- **Chapter 11: The Threat.** Office break-in. One piece of evidence goes missing.
- **Chapter 12: The Missing Witness.** Arthur Pendleton (Assistant) disappears. Encrypted location message received.
- **Chapter 13: Deciphering.** Logic puzzle to crack Arthur's code using Notebook clues.
- **Chapter 14: The Spider's Identity.** Meeting the informant. Demands for security guarantees.
- **Chapter 15: Crossroads of Trust.** Critical choice: Trust the "repentant" dirty cop or the mysterious informant.

### Part IV: The Final Game (Chapters 16–20)
- **Chapter 16: File 'Nemesis'.** Recovering Vane's massive research archive.
- **Chapter 17: Witch Hunt.** Media campaign against the Detective. Every move monitored.
- **Chapter 18: The Final Piece.** Finding the murder weapon or a key audio recording.
- **Chapter 19: The Final Report.** Player assembles the final accusation (Killer, Motive, Accomplice).
- **Chapter 20: The Verdict (Finale).** Consequences of all choices across 20 chapters are revealed.

---

## Endings
- **Ending A (Ideal):** Killer caught, corruption exposed.
- **Ending B (Partial):** Killer caught, but the Mastermind escapes due to mistakes in Chapters 7 & 15.
- **Ending C (Tragic):** Innocent person accused. Real culprits eliminate the Detective.

---

## Side Stories (The Extended Web)
Unlocked after the main story. They seem random but connect back to the Mastermind.
1. **Case 1: Alibi at Midnight.** Stolen necklace lead to the corrupt cop from Ch 8.
2. **Case 2: Code Error.** Corporate spy linked to Atlas Foundation (Ch 6).
3. **Case 3: The Final Canvas.** Poisoned artist linked to offshore accounts (Ch 10).
4. **Case 4: Ghosts in the Net.** Blackmailer using Spider's server proxies (Ch 14).
5. **Case 5: Gray Sector Trail.** Missing man discovered illegal traffic financed by the Mastermind.

## The Secret Chapter 21 (Epilogue)
Unlocked only if all 5 side stories are solved correctly. Allows the player to correct the mistake from Ending B and finally take down the Mastermind for a "Total Victory" (Ending A).

---

## Key Mechanics
- **Action Points (AP):** Limited moves per chapter (introduced in Ch 2).
- **Evidence Files:** Automatic logging of facts.
- **Investigation Wall:** Player pins key leads to filter noise.
- **Notebook (Journal):** Vital for logical puzzles and tracking lies.
- **Branching Interrogations:** Success/Failure rolls based on tone and evidence.
